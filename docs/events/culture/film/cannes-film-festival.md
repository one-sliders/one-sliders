## Latest Verified Status

- Festival dates: 12–23 May 2026; awards ceremony 23 May 2026
- Jury president: Park Chan-wook (South Korea)
- 22 films In Competition; at least 17 have screened as of 22 May
- Awards ceremony is scheduled for 23 May (tomorrow)

1. Fatherland (Pawel Pawlikowski) — 3.3/4
2. Minotaur (Andrey Zvyagintsev) — approx 3.2/4
3. Bitter Christmas (Pedro Almodovar) — strong, ranked near 2nd/3rd
4. All Of A Sudden (Ryusuke Hamaguchi) — 3.1/4
4. Hope (Na Hong-jin) — joint third, approximately 3.1/4
- Fjord (Cristian Mungiu) — dividing critics on Screen grid
