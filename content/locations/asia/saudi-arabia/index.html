Worth seeing

  • Mecca & Medina: The two holiest cities of Islam, the destination of the Hajj pilgrimage for 1.8 billion Muslims.
  • Al-Ula: Ancient Nabataean ruins of Hegra, dramatic rose-red rock formations, and an emerging arts scene.
  • Jeddah Old Town: UNESCO-listed historic centre with coral-stone buildings and vibrant Red Sea waterfront.
