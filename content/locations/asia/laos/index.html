History

1353The Kingdom of Lan Xang emerged, giving the area a defined monarchical state structure.
1707Kingdoms of Luang Prabang, Vientiane and Champasak ruled or strongly influenced the territory before the modern state.
1778Vassals of Siam appears in Laos' historical record.
1893French protectorate placed the territory under outside administration.
1947Unified kingdom ruled or strongly influenced the territory before the modern state.
1953Laos became independent from France, marking a key step in modern statehood.
