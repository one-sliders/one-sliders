City history

1968Yaren District was created in 1968, the same year Nauru became an independent republic.
MakwaYaren was formerly known as Makwa or Moqua, names connected with Moqua Well in the district.
GovernmentYaren became the location of Nauru's parliament, government offices and other national administration buildings.
AirportNauru International Airport is located in Yaren, giving the district its main transport role.
ConstituencyYaren is also a political constituency returning two members to the Parliament of Nauru.
TodayModern Yaren functions as Nauru's de facto capital district, though the country has no official capital city.
