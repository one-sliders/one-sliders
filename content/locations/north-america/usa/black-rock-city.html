Travel Tips

Best time to visit

Accommodation usually means Reno-area hotels before or after the event, not hotels in Black Rock City itself.

Transport notes

Transport is a major part of the trip; confirm vehicle, fuel, water and departure plans early.

Crowds

Expect heavy traffic into and out of the playa around opening and exodus periods.

Weather considerations

Desert weather can swing from intense heat and dust to cold nights and storms.
