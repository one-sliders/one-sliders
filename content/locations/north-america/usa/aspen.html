1879The city's roots are traced to the winter of 1879, when a group of miners ignored pleas by Frederick Pitkin, Governor of Colorado, to return across the Continental Divide to avoid a Ute uprising.
1880The Aspen, Colorado, post office opened on June 7, 1880.
1881Pitkin County was created on February 23, 1881, with Aspen as its first and only seat.
