Best Areas to Stay

The Strip

Best-known resort corridor with casinos, shows, restaurants and easy access to major arenas.

Best for: tourism, nightlife, events
Downtown Las Vegas

Older casino district around Fremont Street with a different nightlife feel and often simpler access.

Best for: nightlife, value
Summerlin

Suburban west-side base near Red Rock Canyon and quieter resort options.

Best for: families, outdoors, quieter stays
Henderson

Southeast metro base with shopping, family stays and road access toward Hoover Dam.

Best for: families, business, road trips
Convention Center area

Practical for trade shows and Formula 1 or arena weekends when central rooms are tight.

Best for: business, events

Travel Tips

Best time to visit

Check resort fees, parking fees and event surcharges before comparing rooms.

Transport notes

Monorail, taxis and rideshare work well on the Strip, but traffic can crawl during major events.

Crowds

Crowds surge for conventions, fight nights, New Year, Super Bowl events and Formula 1.

Weather considerations

Summer heat is extreme; spring and fall are more comfortable for walking.
