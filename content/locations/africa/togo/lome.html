1890But, if the infrastructure set up by the Germans - which consisted of a post office in 1890, the telephone in 1894, the cathedral in 1904, a bank in 1906 and the intercontinental telegraph.
19th centuryThe city was founded by the Ewes and expanded in the 19th century by German, British and African traders, becoming the capital of Togoland in 1897.
1904Lomé benefited from 1904 from a port that made it the only maritime contact point of Togo, ruining its rival, Aného, until then much more important.
