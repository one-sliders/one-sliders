Worth seeing

  • Three Towers: The UNESCO medieval towers of Guaita, Cesta and Montale crown Monte Titano.
  • World's Oldest Republic: Founded in 301 AD, San Marino claims the world's oldest constitutional government.
  • Panoramic Views: The old city overlooks the Adriatic coast, Apennines and even distant peaks on clear days.
