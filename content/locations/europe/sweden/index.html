Sweden

History

c. 800-1050Viking Age trade, raids and settlements connected Sweden with the Baltic, river routes and Byzantium.
1000sChristianization and regional power centers shaped the early Swedish kingdom.
1397The Kalmar Union placed Sweden, Denmark and Norway under one crown.
1523Gustav Vasa became king, ending the union era and marking a national starting point.
1611-1721Sweden rose as a Baltic great power before the Great Northern War ended the empire.
1809Sweden lost Finland to Russia and adopted a new constitution.
1905The union with Norway dissolved peacefully tied Sweden into a larger political structure and changed its constitutional direction.
1995 / 2024Sweden joined the EU in 1995 and NATO in 2024, an important milestone in Sweden's political development.
