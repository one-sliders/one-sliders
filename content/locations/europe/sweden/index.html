Landscape

Sweden has large forest regions, thousands of lakes, Baltic coastlines and mountain areas in the north.

Urban life

Stockholm, Gothenburg and Malmo anchor much of the country's economy, culture and transport network.

Climate

The south is milder and more temperate, while northern Sweden has long winters and subarctic conditions.

Sweden stretches from the Baltic Sea and fertile southern regions to forests, lakes and mountain landscapes in the north. Its cities are strongly connected by rail, ports and digital infrastructure, while much of the country keeps a close relationship to nature, seasonal light and outdoor life.
