Helsingborg city overview

Helsingborg is one of Skane's key Oresund cities, facing Helsingor across the narrowest part of the strait. The city is shaped by medieval fortifications, ferry traffic, port industry, Danish-Swedish border history, coastal parks and a compact centre around Karnan, Norra Hamnen and the waterfront.
