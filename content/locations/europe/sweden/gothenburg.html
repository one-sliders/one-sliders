Gothenburg

Gothenburg is a major city in Sweden, known for its harbor, trams, seafood, music festivals and west coast setting.

Harbor city

Gothenburg grew around its port and remains closely tied to shipping, industry and west coast trade.

Urban life

Trams, cafes, seafood restaurants, parks and compact neighborhoods shape much of the city's rhythm.

Culture

Music, design, science events and festivals give Gothenburg a strong year-round cultural calendar.

Gothenburg sits where the Gota alv reaches the Kattegat, giving the city a maritime identity that runs through its harbor, food culture and public spaces. It is Sweden's second-largest city and a west coast hub for events, education, transport and industry.
