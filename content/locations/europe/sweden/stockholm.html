Water city

Stockholm spreads across islands, bridges and quays between Lake Malaren and the Baltic Sea.

Urban life

Dense neighborhoods, museums, universities, restaurants and offices sit close to parks and waterfronts.

Archipelago

The Stockholm archipelago extends eastward with thousands of islands, ferries and summer communities.

Stockholm combines historic districts, royal institutions, modern business districts and a strong public transport network. Its identity is shaped by water: ferries, bridges, harbors, lakeside walks and nearby islands make the city feel both urban and open.
