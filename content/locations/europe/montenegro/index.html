Worth seeing

  • Bay of Kotor: A drowned canyon mistaken for a fjord, with medieval walled towns inside.
  • Durmitor Park: A UNESCO national park with a glacial Black Lake and skiing in winter.
  • Tara River Canyon: The second deepest canyon in the world, a rafting and hiking destination.
