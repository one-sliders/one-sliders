City history

1895The carpentry business later bought by Ole Kirk Christiansen was founded in Billund.
1916Ole Kirk Christiansen bought Billund Machine Joinery and Carpentry Business, the workshop that later led to Lego.
1934Christiansen named his toy business Lego, anchoring the company identity that would reshape the town.
1964Billund Airport opened to the public after growing from a private runway linked to the Lego company.
1968Legoland Billund opened as the first Legoland park, turning the town into a major family tourism base.
17th centuryBillund is recorded as a settlement with origins in the 17th century in central Jutland.
