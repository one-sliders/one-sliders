Eastern European folk

Fiddle, accordion, choral colour and village-dance memory.

Topic

What it is

Eastern European folk is rich in regional difference. On a topic page it works as an orientation point for fiddle-led melodies, choral texture and compact dance rhythms.

History

Where it comes from

The sound comes from village dances, ritual songs, borderland exchange and professional folk ensembles. It often keeps a strong link between music, food, season and family gatherings.
