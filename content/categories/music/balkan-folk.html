Music topic

Balkan folk

Brass, odd meters, strong voices and celebration energy.

Topic

What it is

Balkan folk covers many local traditions, but the common signal is rhythmic confidence: brass, clarinet, accordion, drums and voices built for dancing and gathering.

History

Where it comes from

Village dance forms, Roma musicians, Ottoman-era instruments and later brass-band traditions shaped a sound that travels easily into festivals and film music.
