Spanish Guitars

Flamenco pulse, classical tone and bright nylon-string colour.

Topic

What it is

Spanish guitar music is direct and physical: fingers, wood, rhythm and melody. It ranges from classical repertoire to flamenco intensity and modern guitar pieces used for warm table settings.

History

Where it comes from

The Spanish guitar became a concert instrument through classical composers and performers, while flamenco developed through Andalusian vocal, dance and guitar traditions. The shared sound is expressive, percussive and highly portable.
