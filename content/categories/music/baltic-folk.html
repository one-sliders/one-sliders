Music topic

Baltic folk

Song festival culture, drones, choirs and earthy dance tunes.

Topic

What it is

Baltic folk is closely tied to singing culture. It can feel ceremonial, earthy or spare, with strong links to seasons, language and choral gathering.

History

Where it comes from

Folk songs, dance tunes and massive song festivals shaped Baltic musical identity. Contemporary artists often keep old instruments and vocal forms in a modern frame.

Quick facts
Origin
Estonia, Latvia and Lithuania
Era
Folk roots with modern choral and revival scenes
Core sound
Choirs, kokle/kankles, fiddle, voice and seasonal songs
Best for
Rye bread, potatoes, forest mood and choral identity
