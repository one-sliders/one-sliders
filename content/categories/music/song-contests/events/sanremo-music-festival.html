Sanremo Music Festival

A signature televised song festival followed for its live performances, competing artists and influence on the Eurovision season.

Overview

Live music contestSanremo combines new songs, televised performances and juried/public voting across a multi-night festival format.
First held in 1951The festival became one of Europe's best-known song contests and helped shape the format that later inspired Eurovision.
Why it mattersArtists, songs, staging and broadcaster decisions are followed closely before, during and after the festival week.
