Jazz

Improvisation, swing, blue notes and city scenes.

Topic

What it is

Jazz is a live music language built on improvisation. It can be intimate club music, big-band swing, festival spectacle or experimental sound, but the point is usually the same: players respond to each other in the moment.

History

Where it comes from

Jazz developed from blues, ragtime, brass bands and dance music in the early twentieth century. It moved through swing, bebop, cool jazz, hard bop, fusion and modern scenes while keeping improvisation at its centre.
