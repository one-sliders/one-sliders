Rock

Guitars, drums, hooks and live-band energy.

Topic

What it is

Rock is the broad guitar-led music topic: bands, riffs, drums, choruses and a live format that works from small clubs to stadiums. It is a useful parent topic for classic rock, indie rock, alternative scenes and big festival headliners.

History

Where it comes from

Rock grew from rhythm and blues, country, gospel and early rock and roll, then expanded through British invasion bands, psychedelic records, arena rock, punk, indie and alternative scenes.
