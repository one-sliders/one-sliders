Music Festivals

Destination lineups, fields, stages and summer travel plans.

Topic

What it is

Music festivals are built around concentrated time: several days, many stages and a location that becomes part of the memory. People search them for dates, lineups, tickets, camping, transport and weather.

History

Where it comes from

Modern festivals grew from folk, jazz and counterculture gatherings into global destination brands. The strongest ones now mix headline acts, local identity, food areas, broadcast clips and travel infrastructure.
