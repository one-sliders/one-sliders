Trance

Melodic electronic build-ups, euphoric drops and big-room emotion.

Topic

What it is

Trance is electronic dance music built around lift: repeated motifs, long builds, emotional breakdowns and releases that feel designed for a crowd moving together.

History

Where it comes from

Trance developed from early European club music, techno, house and ambient influences in the 1990s. It became a global festival sound through dedicated labels, radio shows and superstar DJs.
