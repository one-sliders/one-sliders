Song Contests

Televised competitions where songs become national stories.

Topic

What it is

Song contests sit between music, television and national identity. The format turns a three-minute song into a live voting moment, with rehearsals, fan zones, qualifiers and final-night watch parties shaping the calendar.

History

Where it comes from

The modern template comes from post-war European broadcasting: live orchestras, juries, national broadcasters and later televoting. Eurovision made the format global enough that national finals now behave like events in their own right.
