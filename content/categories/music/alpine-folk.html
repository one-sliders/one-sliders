Alpine folk

Mountain instruments, yodel colour and village-hall warmth.

Topic

What it is

Alpine folk is place music: mountains, valleys, lodges and village halls. It can be traditional, comic, sentimental or festival-ready, but it usually feels rooted in altitude and community.

History

Where it comes from

Yodel, dance tunes, zither music and brass bands shaped the sound across Austria, Switzerland, Bavaria and neighbouring regions. Tourism helped make the style globally recognisable.
