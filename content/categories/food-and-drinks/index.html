Food and Drink

Food and Drink is about how places taste. It follows wine regions, beer halls, street markets, coffee cities, seasonal harvests and the local rituals that make travel feel grounded.

Why it matters

This category should feel sensory and regional: what people eat and drink, where those traditions come from, and how they shape routes, events and city life. Topics should be concrete enough to connect with countries, cities and annual calendars.
