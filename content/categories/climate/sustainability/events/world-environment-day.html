World Environment Day

World Environment Day is the annual UN environment observance held on 5 June, built around public action, policy attention and local events worldwide.

Campaigns, public observances and practical action dates.
HistoryFrom 1974 to a global platform

The observance started after the creation of UNEP and is now used by governments, schools, communities and organisations to focus public attention on environmental protection.

Format / rulesOne fixed date, many local actions

The date stays 5 June. The annual host, theme, public programme and local events can change by edition.

World Environment Day is treated as a broad public-engagement platform rather than a single ticketed venue event.

Notable moments

  • The 2026 host edition is in Baku, Azerbaijan.
  • The 2026 host edition is the next/current edition on 5 June 2026.
