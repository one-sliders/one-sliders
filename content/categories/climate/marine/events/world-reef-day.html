Overview

What World Reef Day is for

World Reef Day is a June 1 awareness day for coral reefs, ocean health and everyday choices that reduce pressure on reef ecosystems.

PurposeTurn reef concern into action

The official campaign frames June 1 as a call to action for consumers, businesses and organizations to protect coral reefs through education and engagement.

What happensReef-safe choices, cleanups and education

Past campaign material has focused on beach cleanups, reef-safe products, sea turtle protection, plastic reduction and coral restoration groups.

Why follow itReefs are fragile ocean infrastructure

Coral reefs support marine life, coastal communities and tourism, but they are highly exposed to warming seas, pollution and damaging consumer choices.

Campaign lensEducation and engagement

Look for toolkit material, reef-safe guidance, partner posts and local ocean or beach actions around the start of World Oceans Month.
