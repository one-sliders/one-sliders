Records and signals

  1. Most Tonys by a musical: Guinness lists The Producers as the musical with the most Tony wins: 12 awards in 2001.
  2. Youngest winner: Britannica notes Frankie Michaels was 11 when he won in 1966.
  3. Composer record: Stephen Sondheim is a central Tony record-holder for original score and composer/lyricist wins.
