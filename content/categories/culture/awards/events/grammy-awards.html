Fun facts Grammy Awards quick facts

Verified context, category quirks and viewing notes that make this award different.

Record vs SongRecord of the Year rewards the recording; Song of the Year rewards songwriting. Same track can win both.
Premiere Ceremony mattersMany genre and craft Grammys are awarded before the televised show.
Awards-season outlierThe Grammys are less about one ceremony city and more about the recording-year map across genres.
