National day

Sweden National Day

Why this day?Gustav Vasa and the 1809 constitution

Sweden celebrates 6 June because Gustav Vasa was elected king on 6 June 1523, often treated as the start of modern Sweden, and because the 1809 Instrument of Government was adopted on the same date.
