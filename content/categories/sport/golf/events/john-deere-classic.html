John Deere Classic 2026

John Deere Classic returns to TPC Deere Run in Silvis, Illinois, the Quad Cities PGA TOUR stop known for aggressive scoring, reachable rough and a closing stretch where birdies decide the week.

WhenJuly 2-5, 2026

Four tournament rounds at TPC Deere Run.

WhereTPC Deere Run

Silvis, Illinois

Defending championBrian Campbell

Course profileTPC Deere Run rewards low scoring
