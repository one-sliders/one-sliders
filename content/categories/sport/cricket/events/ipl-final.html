IPL Final

The IPL Final is the Indian Premier League title match: one night, two playoff survivors and a venue that can reshape travel demand as soon as it is confirmed.

First season2008
FrequencyAnnual
Current edition2027 TBC
Format sizeOne T20 final
HistoryShort, loud and late-deciding

The final is the last match of the IPL season. Finalists are known only after the playoff bracket, so travel and ticket interest usually concentrates late.

Format / rulesT20 title match

Two teams play one Twenty20 match. If the match is tied, the competition rules decide the tiebreak method for that edition.
