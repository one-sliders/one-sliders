Limoncello Spritz

Lemon liqueur, prosecco and soda: bright, sweet and simple enough for a full tray of glasses.

Quick facts

GlassWine glass
MethodBuild over ice
Time3 minutes
GarnishLemon wheel

Ingredients

  • 60 ml limoncello
  • 90 ml prosecco
  • 30 ml soda water
  • 1 lemon wheel

How to make

  1. Fill a wine glass with ice.
  2. Add limoncello and prosecco.
  3. Top with soda water.
  4. Stir gently and garnish with lemon.

Recipe balance

  • Limoncello 33%
  • Prosecco 50%
  • Soda 17%
  • Lemon aroma

Where it matters

Limoncello is strongly connected to southern Italian lemon-growing regions and after-dinner drinking.

Italy

Good to know

ProfileSweet lemon, soft bubbles and a sunny finish.
AdjustAdd more soda if the limoncello is very sweet.
Common missUsing warm limoncello. Keep it chilled.
