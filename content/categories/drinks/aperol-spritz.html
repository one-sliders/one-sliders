Aperol Spritz

A low-strength aperitivo with orange bitterness, prosecco and soda. Built for ice, sunlight and salty snacks.

Quick facts

GlassWine glass
MethodBuild over ice
Time3 minutes
GarnishOrange slice

Ingredients

  • 90 ml prosecco
  • 60 ml Aperol
  • 30 ml soda water
  • 1 orange slice

How to make

  1. Fill a wine glass fully with ice.
  2. Add prosecco, then Aperol.
  3. Top with soda and stir gently once.
  4. Garnish with orange.

Recipe balance

  • Prosecco 50%
  • Aperol 33%
  • Soda 17%
  • Orange aroma

Where it matters

The spritz is closely associated with northern Italian aperitivo culture.

Italy

Good to know

ProfileBittersweet orange, bubbles and a dry finish.
AdjustMore soda makes it lighter; more prosecco makes it drier.
Common missToo little ice. A warm spritz turns flat fast.
