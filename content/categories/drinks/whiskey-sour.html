Whiskey Sour

Whiskey, lemon and sugar. Egg white is optional, but it gives the drink its silky top.

Quick facts

GlassRocks glass
MethodShake
Time5 minutes
GarnishCherry or lemon

Ingredients

  • 60 ml bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 25 ml lemon juice
  • 20 ml simple syrup
  • 15 ml egg white, optional

How to make

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker without ice and shake briefly.
  2. Add ice and shake hard until cold.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass over ice.
  4. Garnish with cherry or lemon.

Recipe balance

  • Whiskey 50%
  • Lemon 21%
  • Syrup 17%
  • Egg white 12%

Where it matters

The Whiskey Sour is part of the American sour family: spirit, citrus and sweetener in balance.

USA

Good to know

ProfileTart, rounded, whiskey-led and silky if egg white is used.
AdjustRye gives spice; bourbon gives vanilla and caramel.
Common missToo much lemon without enough sugar to balance it.
