Cocktail recipe

Irish coffee

Hot coffee, Irish whiskey, sugar and cold cream. Drink through the cream; do not stir it in.

Ingredients

  • 120 ml strong hot coffee
  • 40 ml Irish whiskey
  • 2 tsp brown sugar, or 10 ml syrup
  • 30 ml lightly whipped cold cream
  • Warm handled glass

How to make it

  1. Warm the glass with hot water, then empty it so the drink stays hot.
  2. Add sugar and strong coffee. Stir until the sugar has fully dissolved.
  3. Add Irish whiskey and stir once more so the base is even and hot.
  4. Float cold cream over the back of a spoon. Serve without stirring so each sip passes through the cream.

Serving note

Keep the contrast clear: hot sweet coffee below, cold pourable cream above, and a modest whiskey measure so the drink stays balanced.
