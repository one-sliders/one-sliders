Cocktail recipe

Espresso Martini

Vodka, coffee liqueur and fresh espresso shaken hard into a cold, foamy coupe.

Ingredients

  • 40 ml vodka
  • 30 ml fresh espresso, cooled briefly
  • 20 ml coffee liqueur
  • 10 ml simple syrup, optional
  • Three coffee beans for garnish

How to make it

  1. Chill a coupe glass so the drink lands cold and stays tight.
  2. Brew espresso and let it stop steaming before it hits the shaker.
  3. Add vodka, coffee liqueur, espresso and syrup to a shaker with plenty of ice.
  4. Shake hard for 12 seconds, then fine strain into the glass so the foam sits cleanly on top.

Serving note

The foam is part of the drink. Fresh espresso, enough ice and a hard shake are what give the coupe its pale crema top and coffee-bean finish.
