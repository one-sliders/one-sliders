Wellness topic

Sauna

Sauna events bring together heat, cold water, recovery, design, community bathing and Nordic wellness culture.

The largest sauna events range from congresses and trade fairs to Aufguss championships, winter sauna marathons and public bathing festivals.

Finland is estimated to have about 3.3 million saunas, more than one per two people.
