Wellness

Wellness is slower travel with a purpose: spa towns, saunas, yoga retreats, walking routes, mountain air, cold water, quiet hotels and events built around recovery or movement.

Core lensRecovery
Best seasonYear-round
Place patternNature and resorts
Trip paceSlow
Classic anchorsSauna, spa, yoga, hiking and running all connect body, place and season.
EnvironmentCoasts, forests, mountains, hot springs and quiet rural areas often carry the experience.
Visitor valueA wellness page should make the mood clear: active, restorative, social, silent, warm or cold.
Why it matters

Wellness should feel calm but concrete. It is not only relaxation; it includes heat rituals, movement, retreats, mental reset and destinations where landscape is part of the experience.
