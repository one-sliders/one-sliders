What people compare before booking
Spa break
Is this mostly comfort, treatments and thermal water, or does it include movement and coaching?
Yoga retreat
Beginner-friendly or serious practice? People look for pace, group size, food and silence rules.
Sauna trip
Heat, cold plunge, lake access and local etiquette matter more than a generic hotel spa label.
Active reset
Walking, hiking, running or cycling, with enough recovery time that it still feels restorative.
Quiet reset
Low noise, nature, sleep, digital distance and a realistic schedule are the main decision points.
Wellness festival
People want to know the program, crowd size, accommodation, weather and whether it is substance-free.
