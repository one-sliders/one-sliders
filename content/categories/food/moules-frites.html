Recipe

Moules-frites

Mussels steamed with wine and served with fries.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg mussels
  • White wine
  • Shallot
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Fries

How to make it

  1. Clean mussels and discard any open ones that do not close. Prepare this first and keep the heat moderate; rushing the first stage usually gives uneven texture later.
  2. Cook shallot and garlic, then add wine. Continue gently and check texture rather than only the clock; the dish is ready when the main ingredient is tender and well seasoned.
  3. Steam mussels until open. Continue gently and check texture rather than only the clock; the dish is ready when the main ingredient is tender and well seasoned.
  4. Serve with parsley, broth and fries. Taste once more before serving. Add salt, acid, herbs or a spoon of sauce at the end while the dish is still hot.
