Why It Matters

The Circuit Gilles Villeneuve sits on an island in the St. Lawrence River, making it one of the most scenic and fan-friendly stops on the F1 calendar. Montreal fills up fast race weekend — hotels, flights and grandstand tickets all tighten months in advance.
