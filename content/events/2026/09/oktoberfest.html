Okto­berfest

📅 19 Sep – 4 Oct 2026 📍 Theresienwiese, Munich

The world's largest folk festival. Two centuries of tradition, six million visitors, fourteen beer halls and one city that does it like no other.

16Days
~6MVisitors
14Major tents
~7.7M LBeer poured
Nothing else sounds, smells or feels like this At noon on 19 September, Munich's mayor taps the first barrel and shouts "O'zapft is!" — It is tapped. From that moment, 14 enormous tents fill with the sound of brass bands, the clink of one-litre steins and six million people from every corner of the world sharing the same long communal tables.
