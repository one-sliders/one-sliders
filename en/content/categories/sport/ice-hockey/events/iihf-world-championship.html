HistoryOlympic roots, annual identity

The first world championship was connected to the 1920 Olympic tournament in Belgium. The event later settled into a spring world-title rhythm.

Format / rulesGroups, then knockouts

Top-division teams play group games, the best sides move into quarter-finals, and the final weekend decides the medals.
