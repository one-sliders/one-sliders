What to follow

  • The main Competition is where Cannes awards the Palme d'Or.
  • The 2026 main jury is chaired by Park Chan-wook.
  • The red carpet matters because Cannes is both a film festival and a global media stage.
  • The Marché du Film runs alongside the festival for industry meetings, sales and distribution.
  • Results and award winners can be shown on OneSliders when available.

Why it matters

Cannes is useful as a OneSliders event because dates, place, awards, film premieres, industry attention and travel pressure all converge in a compact city. For visitors and film followers, the practical questions are what is screening, who is on the jury, when awards happen and how Cannes connects to the wider film calendar.
