What to know

  • Wimbledon is the grass-court Grand Slam and one of tennis's four majors.
  • The event is built around singles, doubles, juniors, wheelchair draws and invitational events.
  • Centre Court and No. 1 Court carry the biggest matches, but early-week value often comes from outside courts.
  • Weather, grass wear and short points can change the tournament rhythm quickly.
