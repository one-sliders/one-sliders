What is a OneSlider?

It is not a long article or a dashboard. It is a visual snapshot with images, short facts and charts that can change as the page moves from preview to results.

  1. 1General: the evergreen story, format, records and context.
  2. 2Year: dates, place, countdown and practical planning answers.
  3. 3Parts: schedule, live status, results and the pieces that change.
